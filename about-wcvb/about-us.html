    About TCNJ's
    Women's Club Volleyball Team

    Our Practice Team

    Our club's Practice Team is open to all skill levels and those interested in playing volleyball. These members are invited to join our open practices and other fun volleyball events. Potential members are able to join by contacting us or attending our club's table during TCNJ's Involvement Fair held at the beginning of the Fall and Spring semesters.

    Contact Us

    Our Travel Team

    Our Travel Team is a group of club members looking to play competitively against other college club teams. These members are required to tryout at the beginning of the Fall semester. These individuals then have the opportunity to be chosen to attend tournaments and or Nationals as well as our closed practices.
